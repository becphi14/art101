Lab 13: Loops

Challenge

The purpose of this lab is to practice and experiment with loops, and have the output show up on a webpage. We want to use our knowledge of both loops and conditionals to print the words "Fizz", "Buzz", "Boom", and any combination of the three in place of certain numbers.

Problems

We definitely had a lot of problems with this lab, but were finally able to get it after hours of trying! Once we figured out that we needed to have all of our rules be "if" conditionals, and not "else if", the words and numbers printed on the page. We struggled with how to get the output to print onto the page, and not just in the console, but with a little help we figured it out.
