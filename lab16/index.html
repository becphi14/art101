Lab 16: JSON and APIs

Challenge:

This lab's challenge was to access an API, locate the endpoint, and have it output onto a webpage. This involves experimenting with JSON, and understanding how to manipulate and use it.

Problems:

We had quite a few problems with this lab, most of which led back to our variable not being defined correctly. Once we figured out how to define the empty variable, we could modify it the way we wanted to within the different functions. Once explained, this lab was not too hard beyond just figuring how to structure the functions and variables.
